Are you a true Ina Garten fan?

Perhaps better known as the Barefoot Contessa, chef, author and TV host Ina Garten is a well-loved cooking aficionado known for her New England style, penchant for entertaining and cooking-made-simple approach. While some may simply know Garten for her cooking, she had a high-profile career in the White House before entering the food world. Take this quiz to test your Ina Garten knowledge!
